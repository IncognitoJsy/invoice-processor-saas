Refund Policy

Last updated: January 2025

1. Free Trial

GoZappify offers a free trial period so you can evaluate the Service before committing to a paid subscription. No payment is taken during the trial period, so no refund is applicable.

2. Monthly Subscriptions

Our subscriptions are billed monthly in advance. Because you have the opportunity to evaluate GoZappify during the free trial period, we generally do not offer refunds for subscription payments.

However, we want you to be satisfied with our Service. If you experience significant technical issues that prevent you from using the Service, please contact us within 7 days of your payment and we will review your case for a potential refund or credit.

3. Cancellation

You may cancel your subscription at any time through your account settings. Upon cancellation:

  • You will retain access to the Service until the end of your current billing period
  • No refunds will be provided for the remaining days in your billing period
  • Your account will not be charged again after cancellation

4. Invoice Top-Up Credits

Purchased invoice top-up credits are non-refundable once added to your account. Top-up credits do not expire and will remain available if you cancel and later re-subscribe.

5. Exceptional Circumstances

We may, at our sole discretion, offer refunds or credits in exceptional circumstances, including but not limited to:

  • Extended service outages caused by us
  • Billing errors on our part
  • Duplicate charges

6. How to Request a Refund

If you believe you are entitled to a refund, please contact us at support@gozappify.com with:

  • Your account email address
  • The date of the charge
  • The reason for your refund request

We will respond to refund requests within 5 business days.

7. Chargebacks

If you have a concern about a charge, please contact us first before initiating a chargeback with your bank or card provider. We are committed to resolving billing issues fairly and promptly. Initiating a chargeback without first contacting us may result in suspension of your account.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Refund Policy, please contact us at:

Email: support@gozappify.com
