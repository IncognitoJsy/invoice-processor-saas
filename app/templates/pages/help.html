Getting Started

  1. Sign up for a free 7-day trial - no credit card required
  2. Connect your QuickBooks account during setup
  3. Configure your markup percentages in Settings
  4. Upload your first invoice and watch the magic happen!

We currently support invoices from major UK electrical suppliers including:

  • YESSS Electrical
  • CEF (City Electrical Factors)
  • Wholesale Electrics

We're constantly adding support for more suppliers. Contact us if you need a specific supplier added.

GoZappify accepts PDF invoices up to 16MB in size. This includes consolidated invoice PDFs that contain multiple invoices - our AI will automatically split and process each one separately.

Billing & Subscriptions

Your allowance resets at the start of each billing period.

QuickBooks Integration

During setup (or from Settings), click "Connect to QuickBooks" and sign in with your QuickBooks credentials. You'll be asked to authorize GoZappify to access your account. Once connected, you can sync invoices with one click.

When you sync an invoice to QuickBooks, GoZappify can create:

  • Bills - The supplier invoice as a payable
  • Products/Services - Line items with your markup applied
  • Customer Invoices - Ready to send to your customers
  • Estimates - For quotes you want to send

Xero integration is coming soon! QuickBooks is currently fully supported. If you'd like to be notified when Xero is available, please contact us at support@gozappify.com.

Account & Security

Yes. We take security seriously:

  • All data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS) and at rest
  • We use secure OAuth connections to QuickBooks
  • Passwords are hashed and never stored in plain text
  • We don't share your data with third parties

Click "Forgot Password" on the login page and enter your email address. We'll send you a link to reset your password. The link expires after 1 hour for security.
