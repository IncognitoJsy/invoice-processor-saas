Frequently Asked Questions

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes! You can cancel your subscription at any time. You'll keep access until the end of your billing period.

What counts as an invoice?

Each individual invoice extracted counts toward your limit. If you upload a PDF with 5 consolidated invoices, that counts as 5 invoices.

What happens if I hit my limit?

On Basic, you can purchase additional invoices at £0.50 each, or upgrade to Pro for unlimited invoices.

Do purchased top-up invoices expire?

No! Any extra invoices you purchase never expire and will carry over between billing periods.

What accounting software do you support?

We fully support QuickBooks Online and Xero. More integrations are coming soon!
